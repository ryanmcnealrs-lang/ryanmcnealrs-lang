## 👋 About Me

I'm **Dr. Ryan McNeal** — a U.S. Air Force veteran, Information Systems Security Officer (ISSO), and cybersecurity practitioner with a Doctorate in Information Technology from Capella University. My career spans multiple Department of Defense (DoD) agencies, where I've built hands-on expertise across vulnerability management, application security, risk management frameworks, and compliance operations.

Beyond the technical work, I'm committed to mentorship — supporting high school seniors transitioning to college, college students entering the workforce, and aspiring cybersecurity professionals navigating their careers. I aspire to be an author and speaker focused on cybersecurity, leadership, mentorship, and the kind of growth that only comes from working through real challenges.

> *Cybersecurity is my job. My purpose is in helping others.*
